Pocahontas Water & Sewer Transmission Main

Location:

Pocahontas, AR

Engineer:

DeClerk-Throesch Engineering

Client:

Pocahontas Water & Sewer Systems

This project, completed for the City of Pocahontas, involved the excavation for new water and sewer mains, which included handling, laying, testing, and disinfection of PVC and ductile iron water pipes and fittings, as well as backfilling for water lines, sewer lines, and related structures. Additional scope items included gate valves and valve boxes, fire hydrant assemblies, tapping sleeves with valves and boxes, and the installation of a chlorine analyzer system. Sewer work encompassed the handling, laying, and testing of PVC gravity pipes and fittings, manhole installation, and encasement piping for water and sewer lines.
