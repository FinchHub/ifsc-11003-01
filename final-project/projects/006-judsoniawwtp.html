Judsonia WWTP Improvements

Location:

Judsonia, AR

Engineer:

Salt Engineers

Client:

Judsonia Water Department

Conducted for the Judsonia Water Department, this project involved the construction of new earthen levee embankments and improvements to lagoon containment. Work included the installation of new rip-rap erosion protection and the rehabilitation of existing rock media. Floating lagoon baffles were added to improve treatment flow, along with new floating surface aerators and control panels. An ultrasonic algae control unit was integrated, and a precast concrete chlorine contact chamber was installed for final disinfection.
