Little Maumelle WRF 2025 Asset Renewal

Location:

Little Rock, AR

Engineer:

N/A

Client:

Little Rock Water Reclamation Authority

This project entailed a comprehensive rebuild of the chain and flight systems for Final Clarifiers #2, #3, and #4 at the Little Maumelle Water Reclamation Facility. In addition, four influent final clarifier gates, two influent bio selector gates, and thirteen actuators were replaced, along with the Odor Control Y-Gate actuator and a 12-inch RAS Meter Pit valve.
