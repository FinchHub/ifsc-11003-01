Little Rock Zoo Stormwater Upgrades

Location:

Little Rock, AR

Engineer:

Pollution Management, Inc.

Client:

City of Little Rock

Project work included the installation of inlet filters at both existing and proposed stormwater inlets, along with two new hydrodynamic separators. An existing wash-down pipe was rerouted to the sanitary sewer system, and approximately 200 linear feet of existing stormwater system were replaced with new piping, grate inlets, and junction structures to ensure improved drainage and water quality management.
