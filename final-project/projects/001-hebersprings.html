Heber Springs WTP Improvements

LOCATION:

Heber Springs, AR

ENGINEER:

Salt Engineers

CLIENT:

Heber Springs Water & Sewer

The Heber Springs WTP Improvements project involved the installation of two new Aria-Filtra microfiltration membrane skids, along with all required tanks, piping, valves, control panels, and related ancillary equipment. New process piping and valves provided by the membrane supplier were installed, supplemented by the furnishing and installation of drain and process piping. Additional modules were also installed on the existing membrane skids.
